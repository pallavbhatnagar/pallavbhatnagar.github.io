UX strategist & designer.

H, I'm Pallav, a 25 year old, self taught versatile UX designer who designs to solve problems, to empower people, and to create delightful user experiences. I can polish your messy information architecture, brainstorm on lo-fi concepts or focus on system logic using user flows and wireframes. Through my work, I strive to humanize complex ideas and questions, bridging the gap between people and technology.



Being a kid in the 1990s was the most exhilarating affair for me, with the up and coming technology and the introduction of it all in India. In a way, we grew with the tech, and my computer became my best friend.

The anticipation and imagination of what this tech behind computer is and what it could be, drove me to my graduation in Engineering, but I soon realized that as had been for years, Design was my ultimate calling. From posters and banners to illustrations and basic websites I had been designing since my teenage, as a freelancer but it was during my grad years that my exposure to this world maximized and I could see, clear as day, my path ahead.


From Engineering to UX Design

My stint with Engineering might have been only four years long, but my work in, with and around Computer Science was, is and will always be ongoing. However, alongside my conception of all things computers, I always believed that design is something which can create a difference and experienced it first-hand during my year, working as a Visualizer for a national newspaper after my engineering. It was during the COVID-19 pandemic, that I transformed, upgrading my skills in learning more and more about the Human-Computer Interface, striving everyday to ease the complexities & problems of businesses via design.

I realized that to become an effective problem solver, it is not only crucial to compute & design for user needs but to also comprehend the bigger picture of how a product fits into a company's set of goals, mission, strategies and brand – this made the process of discerning user perspectives as well as the emotions that influence their interaction with digital products not just increasingly enlightening, but also immensely enjoyable to me.


Away from keyboard

For me design is what comedy is for Seinfeld; which drives itself by being simple and consistent. An avid comic reader with a love for all things sci-fi when I am not designing I can be found as a noted Alternative Music junkie amongst my humble group of friends humming along the classics. Be it listening to music or making some of my own, I’m always on it. A space and Quantum Physics aficionado, I love chilling with Netflix and my library boasts of a short, yet very selective collection of books ranging from "Everything That Remains" by Joshua Fields & Ryan Nicodemus, to "Into The Time" by Stephen Hawking.

Experience

With 2+ years of experience as a ux designer, interaction designer, information architect, and similar role, working within a brand development, digital, or full service creative environment. I have experience working with teams to brainstorm and well aware of whiteboard concepts. I can create wireframes at varying levels of fidelity, and enjoy prototyping ideas – it helps me to think through interaction models and perspectives.
