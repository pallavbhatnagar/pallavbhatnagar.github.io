SAAS

Push Email Service Web Application

As a third party product designer for WealthDesk, I was responsible for designing their Emailer & Basket sharing CRM to improve & increase the amount of monthly & daily subscription users.



Concept refinement through iterative processes

I was the preferred UX consultant on a series of projects for the WealthDesk to improve the WealthDesk IT infrastructure. Focused on emailers and Basket sharing functionality by Advisors/Brokers, this was month project.

The Challenge
Emailers are sent for several reasons, but the underlying objective is the same: to convince your prospect or customer to click the notification and take the desired action.

01 — USER INSIGHTS

Identifying pain points.

To understand how the brokers giving a call to their client could be improved, I conducted qualitative interviews with four professional brokers & few users as well. The sessions were 1 - 1.5 hours long and were facilitated as open-ended interviews. Each session focused on specific user scenarios that the participants were familiar with. The participants would walk us through their interactions and pain points.

02 — DESIGN INSPIRATION

The Interface

To remedy the pain points identified in the user research, I explored a range of concepts and ideas. I iteratively qualified the concepts by sharing them with critical stakeholders who critiqued the work. For each iteration, new details and vital aspects were added to the wireframes.

References


Early references for creating more cohesive dashboard


03 — Final SET of Designs

Model for user dashboard

One of the significant issues the lack of flexibility in user profiles. As a business owner of a small business, it might be useful to use the same login credentials that is used as a citizen, whereas larger organizations may require all employees to use a company profile. The wording and interfaces in the current infrastructure are highly technical and are hard to understand for most people.

To deal with this, I created a new model for the relationship between a person and her user profiles. The final result is a concept for how to implement a coherent login flow across different portals. The emphasis was on recognizability, flexibility in profile selection, and adaptability to all platforms.






Conceptual model for my proposed dashboard concept.

WealthDesk is now finishing their broker integrations with different digital brokers for the next generation of the digital trading. Within a few years, I hope to see the principles and concepts integrated in the new solutions.
